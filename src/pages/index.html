Legends of Indian Cinema

Satyajit Ray

Satyajit Ray, one of India's most celebrated filmmakers, was known for his profound storytelling and realistic depiction of Indian life. His masterpieces include the "Apu Trilogy," "Charulata," and "Pather Panchali," which won numerous international awards.

Learn More

Guru Dutt

Guru Dutt was an iconic figure in Indian cinema, known for his poetic storytelling and innovative filmmaking. Films like "Pyaasa" and "Kaagaz Ke Phool" are considered classics.
